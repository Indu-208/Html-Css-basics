Nature is the most beautiful gift on Earth. It includes trees, plants, rivers, mountains, animals, birds, and all living things around us. Nature provides us with fresh air, clean water, food, and shelter. It helps maintain the balance of the environment and supports life on our planet. Spending time in nature makes people feel peaceful, happy, and refreshed. We should protect nature by planting more trees, reducing pollution, and using natural resources wisely. Conserving nature today will ensure a healthy and beautiful world for future generations.
